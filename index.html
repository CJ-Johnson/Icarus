Icarus An imperative programming language

Heads up! Icarus is still very much a work in progress. Syntax and semantics may change without warning.

Design philosophy

Mental continuity

Mental continuity is the idea that a small change how you think about your program ought to result in a small change in the code being written.

Simplicity over uniformity

The rules for a language should be simple. This is often achieved by applying the same rules uniformly in many situations. While this does generally simplify a language it can be taken too far.

Simplicity over familiarity

Simple does not mean familiar. We're not afraid to try something new if it's an improvement upon old. We trust that programmers are smart enough to pick it up quickly

Concurrency

A language without a good concurrency model cannot thrive in today's world.
